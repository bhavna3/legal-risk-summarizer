SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, by and between ABC Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("Client"), and XYZ Services Inc., a California corporation ("Provider").

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings. This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.

11. NOTICES
All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above or such other addresses as the parties may designate in writing.

12. SEVERABILITY
